A little bit about me:

Well, hey there!

I’m Amber Sharpe, a UI/UX Designer and Front-end Developer. I design with my end users in mind (including the great developers on my team). I graduated from Nashville Software School's Developer/Designer six month bootcamp in 2018 and have never looked back.

I love my work and am currently employed as a UX Developer at HealthStream, Inc. In my tenure at HealthStream, I have been on a wide variety of projects. I've gotten the opportunity to design applications and websites in Figma and Sketch, develop websites and React applications, and conduct usability studies and user research. I was also appointed the Google Analytics admin and guru, due to my affinity for data analytics.

I live in Nashville with my husband, Spencer, and our cranky, little dachsund, Mason. I love cooking and rarely make the same recipe twice. I even devoted a special Instagram account to my endeavors. Otherwise, you’ll probably find me doing crosswords, indoor cycling, hanging out with friends, or let’s be honest... watching shows on Netflix.
